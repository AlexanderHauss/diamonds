WHAT IS A COOKIE?

A cookie is a piece of information placed on your computer by the server of the site you are visiting. It contains several data items: the name of the server that placed it; an identifier in the form of a unique number, possibly an expiry date, etc.

This information is sometimes stored on your computer in a simple text file which the server accesses in order to read and record information.

THE ROLE OF COOKIES ON Mysuelly.com

Cookies play an essential role in the way that Mysuelly.com operates.

Their main purpose is to improve your experience on the site.

Some cookies are used to remember your preferences and certain information about your browsing on our Website (pages viewed, etc.).

For example, Mysuelly.com uses cookies to remember your shopping cart so that you can make your purchases online.

The cookies issued by our company are used in particular to improve the presentation of the site and the information about the products offered (aimed at making this information more relevant based on the information provided by the user).

Cookies also help to better adapt and personalize the offers proposed to you.

We use several types of cookies:

Cookies that enable you to identify yourself and consequently to access your account, your shopping cart, etc.

Cookies used to remember the elements that open up on the site in the form of windows (or other elements) and which you have already viewed on a previous visit.

Cookies which help to adapt the presentation of the site to your computer's display preferences.

Cookies which help to establish visit statistics.

Cookies which help to evaluate the overall use of the Website by users.

Cookies which help to generate offers based on user interests.

Using them, we can also propose an appropriate selection of products and services.

Other cookies are issued by third parties, and there is no exhaustive list of third party cookies because they are constantly evolving.

They help to target your interests based on the different pages visited in order to offer appropriate, relevant advertisements.
